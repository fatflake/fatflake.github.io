I am doing my PhD under the supervision of Jörg Lücke in the Computational Neuroscience and Machine Learning Group at FIAS. My research interests are machine learning, Bayesian statistics, and neuroscience.

I completed my M.S. in Computer Science (Informatik) at the Max Planck Institute for Biological Cybernetics with the Universität Tübingen, on semi-supervised Kernel CCA applied to fMRI data with supervision from Andreas Bartels, Matthew Blaschko, Arthur Gretton, and Christoph Lampert. Here is my old homepage at the MPI.
